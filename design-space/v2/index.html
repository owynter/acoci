About ACOCI

The Anguilla Chamber of Commerce & Industry is a non-profit membership organisation representing the private sector in Anguilla. For over four decades we have championed enterprise, advocated for sound policy, and equipped our members with the tools, training, and connections to thrive.

Today, more than 180 member businesses across hospitality, professional services, retail, construction, and beyond rely on ACOCI to be their unified voice.

Anguilla’s economy is changing fast. In her latest letter, the ACOCI President shares how the Chamber is investing in the next chapter for our members — from a renewed advocacy agenda to expanded training, the Backing Winners programme, and a new push on regional partnerships through Chamber Link.
