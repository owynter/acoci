Member Benefits

Why Join?

Joining ACOCI gives your business a front-row seat — and a voice — in shaping Anguilla's economic future. Here's what you can expect as a member of the Chamber.

  • Economic Influence

    A stronger private sector means more opportunity — for everyone.

    • Have a say in the policies that shape your operating environment.
    • Connect with the operators, advisors, and institutions running Anguilla's economy.
  • Advocacy & Policy

    The Chamber represents members to government on legislation and regulation that affects how you do business.

  • Insights & Research

    Quarterly business pulse reports, member surveys, and economic briefings — so you're not flying blind.

  • Talent & Workforce

    Workshops, training programmes, and the connections to attract and retain skilled people on-island.

  • Visibility & Engagement

    Member directory listings, event sponsorships, and a platform to be seen by the rest of the Chamber community.
