The chamber, in plain terms.

The Anguilla Chamber of Commerce & Industry is a non-profit, member-funded organisation representing licensed businesses across every sector of the island’s economy.

We exist to do three things: advocate for sound, business-friendly public policy; convene the people who run Anguillian businesses with each other and with government; and equip our members to grow their operations through programs, training, and a working network.

This pillar tells you how we’re structured, who runs the chamber, what our committees do, and where we’re headed.
