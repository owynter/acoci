Color exploration

Pillar color palette

Eight candidate hero colors named for Anguillian places, organised lightest to deepest. Every color is traceable to a brand source — either directly logo-sampled, or an established secondary token from the v4 brand guide, or a Mission gradient stop. Nothing invented.

Each shown as a hero band with the standard wave and a sample title, eyebrow, and lede — the same chrome the pillar landing pages will use. Body content sits on white below the wave (per spec).

Pick four to assign to the four pillars: Membership, News & Events, Resources, and Your Chamber. Anything you don’t pick can still live in the palette as a token for accent use.
