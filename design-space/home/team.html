Your Chamber

The people behind ACOCI.

ACOCI is governed by a board of directors elected from our member businesses, supported by an executive committee and the chamber team. Together they set the agenda, advocate for the private sector, and run the day-to-day work of the chamber.

The Board of Directors is the volunteer leadership of the Anguilla Chamber of Commerce & Industry. Directors are elected by the membership and serve staggered terms.
