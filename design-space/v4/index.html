About ACOCI

Representing every licensed business on the island.

Founded in 1981, the Anguilla Chamber of Commerce & Industry is the premier private-sector body on the island. We connect businesses with government, provide resources for growth, and champion a competitive, sustainable economy.

What membership unlocks

  • Business Networking

    Connect with fellow members, government officials, and regional partners at curated events throughout the year.

  • Resources & Advocacy

    Access guides, templates, and policy support — and have your voice heard at government consultations.

  • Directory Listing

    Get found by residents, visitors, and investors searching the official ACOCI Business Directory.
